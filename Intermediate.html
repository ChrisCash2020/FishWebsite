Intermediate




Decide on a Fish type

When you feel comfortable enough with beginner level betta fish you can advance to fish such as Cichlids that grow to an average size of 2-14+ inches and display interesting behaviors and personalities



What and How much to feed you Fish

Cichlids diet consists of flakes, pellets, frozen or live food. You need to Feed them an amount they couldn't eat in 1 to 2 minutes twice a day

Environment ideal for your Fish

Environment must be appropriately sized about 10-29+ gallons depending on species, Water quality must be stable with a water temperature of 72-82 degrees Farenheit. Avoid overcrowded conditions because they can cause stress and disease to fish



Cleaning routine for fish

Install and check filter once a day, check the water quality once a week,change 10-25% of the total volume of water every 2-4 weeks, or as needed
