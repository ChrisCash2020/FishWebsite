Advanced




Decide on a Fish type

Previously all the fish mentioned were freshwater fish but when you become more knowledgeable you can acquire saltwater fish such as the Harlequin Sweetlips. They grow to an average size of 28 inches and have a low survival rate



What and How much to feed you Fish

Sweetlips diet consists of live shrimp, black worms, frozen or live. They can be very picky eaters and need constant variations in food types and you also need to feed them 2-3 times a day

Environment ideal for your Fish

Environment must be appropriately sized about 170+ gallons depending on species. They have water parameters such as: Temperature: 72°F to 78°F Carbonate hardness: 8 to 12dKH pH: 8.1 – 8.4 Salinity 1.020 – 1.025 must be stable with a water temperature of. Avoid housing them with aggressive fish and smaller fish



Cleaning routine for fish

Install and check filter once a day, check the water quality once a week, use an aquarium vacuum once a week when younger and multiples times a weeks as they grow bigger
