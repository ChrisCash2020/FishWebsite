Begineer




Decide on a Fish type

The types of pet fish are endless but for someone starting out Its recommended to begin with betta fish because of their low care level and their average size is 2.5 inches



What and How much to feed you Fish

Bettas diet consists of flakes, pellets, freeze-dried bloodworms. You need to Feed them an amount they couldn't eat in 1 to 2 minutes; also avoid overfeeding since it can foul the water

Environment ideal for your Fish

Environment must be appropriately sized about quarter to gallon tank, bettas prefer water with little or no current, and need a water temperature of 72-82 degrees Farenheit. Male bettas must be kept individually because of the aggressiveness whereas female bettas can be housed with other community fish



Cleaning routine for fish

Clean the aquarium tank at least once a week by removing all the foul minerals either indivually with a drainer or with aquarium vacuum, next clean the walls of the aquarium with uused scub(no chemicals), and finally add clean room temperature tap water with tank conditioner
